Résumé Assignment

Due: September 11 by 11:59 PM ET • Submission: PDF uploaded to Canvas • Weight: 10% of course grade

Purpose

Résumés have a long history going back to at least Leonardo da Vinci, who wrote a letter to Ludovico il Moro, Duke of Milan, summarizing his many talents c. 1483 - 1485. 

For this assignment, prepare or revise a professional résumé suitable for use in a job, internship, research, or graduate-school application context. This assignment is intended to produce a polished professional document that you can continue refining and using beyond this course.

Your résumé should present your education, experiences, skills, and accomplishments clearly and strategically for the kinds of opportunities you may pursue after graduation.

Note: For the differences between a résumé an academic CV, check out the following link.

What to Submit

  • A completed or revised résumé.
  • The file must be submitted as a PDF.
  • Upload the PDF directly to this Canvas assignment by the deadline.
  • If you used generative AI while developing or revising your résumé, include the required AI Usage Statement.

Expectations

Your résumé should be professional, readable, and carefully edited. While the precise content will vary, strong résumés typically:

  • Use clear organization and consistent formatting.
  • Highlight relevant education, work, research, leadership, service, projects, or technical experience.
  • Describe experiences in a concise, accomplishment-oriented way.
  • Use language appropriate for professional or academic opportunities.
  • Contain no obvious spelling, grammar, or formatting errors.

Note: The résumé should reflect your own work and professional history. Peer review, instructor feedback, and reasonable collaboration are welcome, but the final submitted document must be your own.
